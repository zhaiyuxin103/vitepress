::: tip
These steps are based on **Windows**. Guides for other operating systems will be added later.
:::

1. Press `Win + X` to open Settings → Time & Language → Language, then click **Options**.

   ![file](/images/如何输入直角引号（「」和『』）/option_1.png)

2. Click the **Options** button shown below.

   ![file](/images/如何输入直角引号（「」和『』）/option_2.png)

3. Choose **Dictionary and self-learning**.

   ![file](/images/如何输入直角引号（「」和『』）/ciku.png)

4. Select **Add or edit custom phrases**.

   ![file](/images/如何输入直角引号（「」和『』）/add_phrase.png)

5. Under **User-defined phrases**, click **Add**.

   ![file](/images/如何输入直角引号（「」和『』）/add_user_phrase.png)

6. In the pop-up window, enter the corner quotes—「」 and 『』—along with the pinyin shortcut you prefer.

   ![file](/images/如何输入直角引号（「」和『』）/add_「」png.png)

   ![file](/images/如何输入直角引号（「」和『』）/add_『』.png)

7. Once saved, the custom entries should look like this:

   ![file](/images/如何输入直角引号（「」和『』）/success.png)
